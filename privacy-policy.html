Privacy Policy - Daily Habit Flow

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Daily Habit Flow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Daily Habit Flow does not require user accounts or registration. We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers through the App.

2.2 Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored locally on your device:

  • Habit Data: Names, colors, frequencies, and completion history
  • Streak Information: Current streaks and best streaks
  • Reminder Settings: Notification times and preferences
  • App Preferences: Theme settings (light/dark mode)
  • Achievement Progress: Unlocked achievements and progress

Important: All this data remains on your device. We do not have access to it, and it is never transmitted to our servers.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Third-party data

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we do not use your data for any purpose. All habit data is stored locally on your device and used solely by the App to provide its functionality.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure data storage systems. We do not maintain any servers or databases containing your information.

4.2 Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS device security features
  • Local encryption provided by Apple's frameworks
  • No network transmission of your data

4.3 Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through the Settings screen in the App. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Apple Services

Daily Habit Flow uses the following Apple services:

  • App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases
  • UserNotifications: For local reminder notifications (data stays on device)
  • SwiftData: For local data storage (data stays on device)

These services are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

5.2 No Third-Party Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

6. In-App Purchases

When you make an in-app purchase:

  • Payment is processed by Apple through your Apple ID
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • Apple may collect transaction data in accordance with their Privacy Policy
  • Purchase history is managed by Apple

7. Notifications

Daily Habit Flow uses local notifications to remind you of your habits. These notifications are:

  • Generated and stored locally on your device
  • Not transmitted to us or any third party
  • Managed entirely by iOS
  • You can disable them in iOS Settings at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Daily Habit Flow is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights

9.1 Access and Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • View all your data within the App
  • Modify or delete habits at any time
  • Delete all data through Settings
  • Export data (premium feature)

9.2 Data Portability

You can export your habit data (premium feature) to transfer it to another device.

9.3 Deletion

You can delete all data at any time. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected (we collect none)
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed (we do not sell or disclose)
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (not applicable - we do not sell)
  • Access your personal information (stored locally on your device)
  • Request deletion of personal information (delete through App Settings)

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in the App
  • Rectification: Modify your data within the App
  • Erasure: Delete all data through App Settings
  • Data Portability: Export your data (premium feature)
  • Object to Processing: Not applicable - we do not process your data
  • Withdraw Consent: Delete the App to remove all data

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
